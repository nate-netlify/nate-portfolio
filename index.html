About

I’m a driven creative with a background in video and marketing, as well as a knack for brand growth. I launched and developed a brand of my own in post-production, videography, and social management, specifically in the sports and esports realms, working with verified content creators & athletes, and high-profile organizations for a combined audience of well over a million. I currently shoot and edit content for athletes at all levels, and I’m passionate about video production in various arenas.
